WHO ARE WE?

Torsan Mussel is the first stop of the street flavors we established under the name of Seyyarhane. We produce the mussels that we receive live from the farms approved by the relevant ministry in our factory, in accordance with the Turkish Food Codex and on a “daily” basis and ship them to our branches. In addition to the traditional taste, we bring a new breath to the street cuisine with our different recipes, always hot, fresh and specially cooked for you whenever you want, with pots of mussels.
